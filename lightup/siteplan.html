Overview

Purpose

The main purpose of this website is to serve as an online bookstore where users will ba able to view, access books.

Targetted audience

  • Demographics:

    • Age: 15-60 years old
    • Interests: Reading, learning, personal growth, and education
    • Education: High school students, college/university students, and young professionals
    • Location: Global, with a focus on English-speaking countries

  • Psychographics:

    • Values education, personal growth, and self-improvement
    • Enjoys reading, learning, and exploring new ideas
    • Tech-savvy and comfortable with online shopping
    • Appreciates convenience, accessibility, and user-friendly experiences

  • User personas: College students,Book lovers, researchers, families, and corporate groups.

Scenerios

  • Options for purchasing e-books or audiobooks
  • Options search for desired books
  • High-quality book covers and author images
  • Recommendations for related books or authors
