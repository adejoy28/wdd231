Overview

Purpose

LightUp bookstore is an online bookstore platform that provides a comprehensive digital marketplace where users can browse, purchase, and access a wide variety of books, complemented by personalized recommendations and an intuitive search system.

Target audience

  • Demographics:

    • Age: 15-60 years old
    • Interests: Reading, learning, personal growth, and education
    • Education: High school students, college/university students, and young professionals
    • Location: Global, with a focus on English-speaking countries

  • Psychographics:

    • Values education, personal growth, and self-improvement
    • Enjoys reading, learning, and exploring new ideas
    • Tech-savvy and comfortable with online shopping
    • Appreciates convenience, accessibility, and user-friendly experiences

  • User personas: College students,Book lovers, researchers, families, and corporate groups.

Scenarios

  • A college student needs to quickly find and borrow required textbooks for their upcoming semester
  • A book lover wants to discover new titles similar to their favorite genres and authors
  • A researcher needs to access academic publications and reference materials
  • A parent is looking for educational books suitable for their children's age group
  • A corporate trainer wants to borrow multiple copies of business books for team development
